What’s inside MotorSphere

Reels

Vertical, high-engagement video feed with creator tools and analytics.

Share

Photo + long-form video with chapters, vehicle & part tags, and affiliate shelf.

Meets

Discover, RSVP, and coordinate—map previews, route planning, organizer tools.

Maps

Waze-style clustering, zoom-to-detail, in-app turn-by-turn navigation.

Market

Vehicles & parts marketplace with filters, saved searches, seller analytics.

Garage

Your builds, badges, AI Influence Score, levels, and reels pinned above your cars.

PTT Walkie-Talkie

Crew channels for cruises; driver-safe big button and background audio.

Safety & Trust

Report, block, auto-moderation, clear track-first community rules.
